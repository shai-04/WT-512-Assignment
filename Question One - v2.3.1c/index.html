Help:

1 Notice the 3x3 grid above? That's where the game takes place. It's basically made up of 3 rows and columns.

2 Once you've wrapped your head around that, choose whether you play as X and your friend as O or vice versa.

3 With that out of the way, you and your friend must now take turns placing your respective symbols on the board (by clicking each square).

4 First player to get 3 of their symbols next to each other (either horizontally, vertically or diagonally) wins the match.

5 Becareful though, if all 9 squares are occupied and none of the players have managed to place 3 symbols next to each other, the match is called a draw and you'll have to start again!

6 If you don't want music or sfx to be played, click the toggle music or toggle sfx buttons to toggle the music and sfx respectively. Alternatively, press ctrl + m to mute all sounds within the browser. Should this not work, try looking for a speaker icon near the tab manager and hover over it for more instructions. However, if your browser doesn't support audio, there will be text indicating this below.
